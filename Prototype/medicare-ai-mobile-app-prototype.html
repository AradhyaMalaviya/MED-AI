Frequently asked
Is this a substitute for a doctor?
No. MediCare AI offers preliminary, informational guidance only. Always consult a licensed healthcare professional for diagnosis or treatment.
Is my data stored?
No. Your inputs are processed in memory on this device for the current session only and are never uploaded to a server.
Which AI models can I choose from?
Random Forest (recommended), Gradient Boosting, and Logistic Regression. You can switch models in the symptom check.
